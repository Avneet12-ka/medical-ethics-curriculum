About the Curriculum

Professional, Ethical and Legal Issues in Malaysian Medical Practice

Curriculum Overview

This curriculum covers professional, ethical, and legal issues relevant to medical practice in Malaysia. The course is designed to provide medical students and practitioners with a comprehensive understanding of ethical principles, professional responsibilities, and legal frameworks governing medical practice in Malaysia.

Through a combination of lectures and interactive case studies, learners will develop the knowledge and skills needed to navigate complex ethical dilemmas in the Malaysian healthcare context. The curriculum emphasizes cultural sensitivity, practical application, and critical thinking.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this curriculum, learners will be able to:

  • Understand the fundamental ethical principles guiding medical practice
  • Apply ethical reasoning to complex medical scenarios in the Malaysian context
  • Recognize legal obligations and boundaries in healthcare delivery
  • Develop professional communication skills for patient interactions
  • Analyze ethical dilemmas using structured frameworks
  • Implement holistic approaches to healthcare that consider cultural and social factors
  • Navigate digital ethics challenges in modern healthcare
  • Maintain professional relationships with colleagues and other healthcare providers
  • Apply ethical principles to medical research
  • Demonstrate accountability and autonomy in professional decision-making

Curriculum Structure

The curriculum consists of two main components:

Lectures (10 sessions)

The lecture series provides theoretical foundations and practical knowledge across a range of ethical, professional, and legal topics relevant to Malaysian medical practice. Each lecture includes real-world case examples, key principles, and practical applications.

Interactive Case Studies (10 sessions)

The case studies allow learners to apply theoretical knowledge to realistic scenarios. Each case study follows this structure:

  • Pre-knowledge materials - Relevant concepts from lecture topics
  • Interactive video/comic scenarios - Application of knowledge
  • Interactive quiz - 15 multiple-choice questions and 5 open-ended questions
  • Post-case discussion - Structured reflection and analysis

Assessment Strategy

The curriculum employs a comprehensive assessment approach:

  • Interactive quizzes within case studies (formative assessment)
  • Post-case discussion write-ups (formative assessment)
  • Final comprehensive assessment covering all lecture topics and case studies

This multi-faceted assessment strategy ensures that learners not only understand theoretical concepts but can also apply them in practical scenarios.

Technical Implementation

The curriculum is delivered through a web-based platform with the following features:

  • Mobile-responsive design for access on various devices
  • Interactive elements using JavaScript
  • Quiz functionality with automated scoring
  • Progress tracking for learners
  • Comic-style interactive scenarios with speech bubbles
  • Character avatars representing different medical roles
  • Click-through panel navigation
  • Interactive decision points with branching scenarios
  • Immediate feedback on ethical choices
